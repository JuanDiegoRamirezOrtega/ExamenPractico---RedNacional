!  CONFIGURACIÓN VTP

! ===========================
!  ENLACES TRONCALES LAN 
! ===========================

! G0/1: Troncal Principal hacia el SWITCH MULTICAPA ANEXO
interface GigabitEthernet0/1
 switchport mode trunk

exit

! G0/2: Puerto para AP

 switchport mode access
 switchport access vlan 34
 no shutdown
exit
